A leather-clad Smokey Robinson (and his Miracles) enthusiastically inspects the Ready Steady Go! studios prior to a mid-'60s British television appearance. Already established at home with hits for Motown colleagues The Temptations (The Way You Do Things) and Mary Wells (My Guy), he's soon to be immortalised in the UK with a string of hits including Tears Of A Clown, I Second That Emotion and Tracks Of My Tears.
