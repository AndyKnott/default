Streamline wit with an impeccable eye for teenage detail made Chuck Berry's slick updates of electric R&B into an American art-form, via gems like Memphis Tennessee, Sweet Little Sixteen and Johnny B Goode. Ironically his biggest hit, the latterday My Ding-A-Ling, is possibly the naffest thing he ever wrote.
