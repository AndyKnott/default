Brian Holland, Lamont Dozier and Eddie Holland, photographed in joint compositional mode, in their award-strewn office in the mid-'60s. Before leaving Motown after a dispute in '67, the label's most celebrated songwriting team had chalked up a stupendous run of hits that included This Old Heart Of Mine, Heatwave, Jimmy Mack and Road Runner.
