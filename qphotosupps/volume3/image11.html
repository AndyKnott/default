Dave Stewart and a bewigged Annie Lennox, only recently freed from their successful but terminally unfashionable band The Tourists, play their first concert as Eurythmics at the Barracuda Club, January 11, 1982. Severely understaffed, Stewart himself looked after the sound and lights while playing a variety of instruments.
