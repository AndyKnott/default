Famously phobic of flying (and incidentally publicising his "Station To Station" tour), David Bowie returns to Britain by boat, May 2, 1976. Clambering into his limo at Victoria, the self-styled Thin White Duke later enlivened the same event with what appeared to be a Nazi salute.
