Marianne Faithfull, Mick Jagger's girlfriend at the time, returns home after a police raid on Keith Richard's Sussex home, Redlands, on February 12, 1967. Jagger and Richard were arrested on drug charges, but the media's interest was equally aroused by the fact that Faithfull had been naked, except for a rug, at the time of the raid. Quizzed in court about the "normality" of such shenanigans, Richard told the prosecution: "We are not old men. We are not worried about your petty morals."
